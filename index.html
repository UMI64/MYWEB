  • Bluetooth Car

    上学期制作的以C51为基础操控芯片的HC-05蓝牙智能小车

    主要原理有串口通讯，定时器中断，超声波避障碍和PWM调速，只不过超声波避障那段程序找不到了···又因为开始学习STM32就没有再管避障了···

    HC-05 based Bluetooth smart car with C51 as control chip.

    The main principle are serial communication, timer interrupt, ultrasonic obstacle avoidance and PWM speed control, but the ultrasonic obstacle avoidance program can not be found···and I began to learn STM32,so there is spare time to do obstacle avoidance.
